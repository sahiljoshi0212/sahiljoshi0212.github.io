Welcome to my web page,

I am a fourth year PhD student and teaching assistant in the Department of Mathematics at IIT Ropar.

My research area is knot theory. Dr. M Prabhakar is my research supervisor. I study knot theory and more generally, knotted graphs in a \(3\)-space from a combinatorial viewpoint. My research interests include the study of invariants of knots and knotted graphs in \(\mathbb{R}^3\) or \(S^3\). Presently, I am focusing on the study of abstract simplicial complexes whose \(0\)-dimensional faces are isotopy classes of either knots, or virtual knots, or spatial graphs. Such complexes are defined by using local surgical operations on planar diagrams of knots or spatial graphs.
